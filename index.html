Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our healthcare solutions

How do I choose the right treatment for my condition?
We recommend starting with our treatment finder tool or consulting with one of our healthcare providers. They can assess your specific needs, medical history, and preferences to recommend the most suitable treatment options for your condition.
Are your treatments FDA-approved and safe?
Yes, all treatments featured on HealthStarTrust are provided through licensed healthcare providers and meet regulatory standards. We only partner with verified medical professionals and FDA-approved medications to ensure your safety and effectiveness.
How much do the treatments cost?
Treatment costs vary depending on the specific medication, dosage, and your insurance coverage. We offer competitive pricing and many patients find our treatments more affordable than traditional pharmacy options. Contact us for a personalized quote.
Do you accept insurance?
We work with most major insurance providers. Our team can help verify your coverage and explore payment options. Many patients save significantly compared to traditional pharmacy costs.
How long does it take to receive my treatment?
Most treatments are delivered within 3-5 business days after your prescription is approved. We offer discreet, free shipping to ensure your privacy and convenience.
Can I speak with a doctor before starting treatment?
Absolutely! We require all patients to consult with licensed healthcare providers before starting any treatment. This ensures the treatment is safe and appropriate for your specific health needs.
What if I have questions or need support?
Our support team is available 24/7 to answer your questions. You can contact us through our website, email, or phone. We're here to ensure you have a positive experience with your healthcare journey.
Are online ED prescriptions covered by insurance?
Some online platforms may accept insurance for ED medications. These sites typically require a copay and/or membership fee. However, many sites do not accept insurance but offer generic versions of ED medications at prices as low as $2 per pill, making them a highly affordable option without coverage.
Does my doctor need to know I'm getting ED treatment online?
It may not be required to inform your general practitioner, but it's highly recommended. Sharing this information helps ensure your overall health is managed properly and avoids potential medication interactions.
Can a sex pill help with psychologically based ED?
Psychological ED doesn’t always respond to a typical sex pill the same way physical ED does. The best sex pill for someone with mental ED might still offer temporary help by improving confidence during sexual activity. Some men find that combining a sex pill with therapy or stress management leads to better results.
Is there such a thing as male enhancement pills?
ED treatments exist, but male enhancement pills for stamina, libido, or size aren’t backed by solid science. Most male enhancement pills are unregulated supplements with mixed results.
